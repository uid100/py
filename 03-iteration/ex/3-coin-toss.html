Exercise 3

Heads or Tails!

In this exercise, you will get some experience with the random number generator in Python. Random numbers are heavily used in many games and simulations for things like dice rolls, dealing cards, flipping a coin, etc.

You will build an application that will flip a coin 20 times, keep a running total of heads and tails.

For each coin toss, display the result (heads or tails).

After the last toss, you will display

  • total number of heads
  • total number of tails
  • and the percentage of each
